Origins

Demon's Souls is a third person action RPG released in Japan on February 5th, 2009 and in North America on October 6th. It was developed by From Software with the help of Sony Japan studio. It was published by Atlus; Namco Bandai Games published the game for Australia and Europe on June 23 and June 25 2010, respectively.
